campus life

School Football Field

Renovated in 2018

I am not such a great fan of football, but since there was no other sport that matched what I was looking for, I decided to opt for Football. Initially, I was very much ignored in the field as I was not so great in it. But, when I passed to 4th Grade, the tables turned. Suddenly, every captain wanted me to be a part of their respective team and become a goalkeeper. And no, this was not to encourage me, instead they had a feeling that I was a good goalkeeper, even though I was thinking the opposite. And since then, football has been one of my favorite sports.


School Swimming Pool

Built in 2012

I used to dread swimming, while my friends considered it "fun time" rather than a sport. I still remember myself waiting anxiously for the bell to ring, so that I could get out. I did not have hydrophobia, but I never liked swimming. It was only after I visited Wonderla, that I started to like swimming and made some progress in learning to swim like my colleagues.
